India's Best online tution at affordable price on Rudraa.org

Rudraa offers personalised LIVE Online tuition classes at your home. In our online tutoring platform, students get to learn from the best teachers who have studied from top colleges and institutes across India. We offer year-long structured courses for CBSE & ICSE board school students, so that students can study entire subject in a structured manner at the comfort of their home. Apart from Regular monthly tuitions, we also provide doubt clearing sessions for students of all grades and boards.

Rudraa's tuition classes are completely online and allows the teacher and student to engage on our website using WAVE platform. All the classes are taken by expert Tutors who ensure to share assignments after completion of every chapter (i.e. regular homework help, subjective & objects tests & quizzes), along with revision notes and formula worksheets. There are regular Parent-Teacher meetings to monitor & track student's academic progress throughout student's learning engagement. All the sessions taken by students on Rudraa are recorded and replays of those sessions are available in student's login account for revision of all the chapters & topics learnt on Rudraa.

We find the RIGHT Tutor to match students' and parents' requirements, and our highly experienced Tutor strives to establish connect with students so that even a first-time student is comfortable with studying online and does not feel the need of taking tuition classes from coaching centers near their home.

Rudraa caters to CBSE and ICSE board from classes 6th to 12th for Maths and Science (Physics, Chemistry & Biology) subjects. Our free reference materials are textbook solutions (NCERT, RD Sharma, HC Verma & etc.), Sample papers for CBSE & ICSE board, important questions and more.
